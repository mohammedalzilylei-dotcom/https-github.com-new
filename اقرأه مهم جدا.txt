The Prophet ﷺ said: "No harm and no reciprocating harm."
Therefore, do not harm anyone with this app; its only purpose is security awareness.
Be careful not to download apps like this from untrusted sources. Even if they don’t show a ban on Google Play, they may still access your photos or erase your data. Anyone can do this with a mobile phone.
